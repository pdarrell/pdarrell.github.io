Design Priniciples

Fitt's Law

Microsoft

Microsoft/microsoft.com

Microsoft displays Fitt's Law by making the Show Now button large and close to the descriptive paragraph so it does not take much effort to click it.

Proximity

VMware

VMware/vmware.com

The way the icons and text are close together makes them seem to be one object and with the heading the whole section is an object to the user.

Whitespace & Clean Design

Dell

Dell/dell.com

The item pictures and labels have just enough whitespace to make them look appealing and appear as one object. And the whole list of items are seen as a single object and are tied together with a similar look and feel.
