Biker Riders at Cabin Exhibit

School children biker ride through the park stopping at the Park's exhibits along the way.

The Park has many exhibits throughout the park and anyone can come and see them.

During specific times the exhibits are manned by exhibit actors who can tell the story of the exhibit and give information about the park and the historical significance of the park and its exhibits.
