Target Audiences

Personas

Travelers

For the traveler in or to the area, they can use the site to know what the weather is in the area so they can plan for their travels.

Outdoor activity people

The people who enjoy outdoor activities, will use the site to find how to they should dress and plan for their activity.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

The traveler outside of the area will logon to the site from home before they start their journey to the area to find out what the weather forecast in the area is during different timeframes, so they can know when the best time to start the journey will be.

Scenario 2

The traveler who lives in the area will login to the site, probably from a phone, to see what the current weather is before they head home from work.

Scenario 3

An outdoor active person will check the site during their activity on a mobile device to know the current weather so they can continue their activity or end it due to the weather changing.

Scenario 4

When an outdoor active person wants to plan an activity, they will log into the site from a home computer to find out the forecast to determine if the weather will be good for the activity.

Scenario 5

When a traveler is journeying to the area, they will use the site to find out the current weather and forecast in the area once they get into the area, from a mobile device.
